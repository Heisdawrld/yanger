Our Story

Built on risk, faith,
and real skill

My name is Mary Nnebuchi Ekeze — but most people call me Yanga Boss. I'm a cosmetologist who spent six years building a reputation at my first location before taking the biggest gamble of my career.

In November 2022, I left everything I knew and moved to Yaba. I rented a small space, thinking that was all I needed. It started rough. Really rough. But God had bigger plans.

Today, Yanga Boss Beaute is a full-service beauty house on Fakunle Street — braids, wigs, relaxers, nails, lashes, makeup, and everything between. Walk in as a client, leave as family. That's not marketing. That's how we built this.
